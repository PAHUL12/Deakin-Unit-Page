About the Course

This course is designed to prepare students for a professional career in Information Technology. It covers essential topics such as ethical practices, teamwork, and communication in the IT industry.

Students will engage in real-world projects, collaborate with peers, and develop the skills necessary to succeed in the fast-paced world of IT.

Hurdle Requirement

Students must pass the hurdle requirement to pass the unit. This includes mandatory attendance and participation in scheduled learning activities.

Failure to meet the hurdle requirements may result in failure of the unit regardless of the total marks achieved.
